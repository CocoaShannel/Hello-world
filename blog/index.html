Information on different types of beer!
  • Lager
    • Smooth-flavored brew made with low stress cold fermentation.
  • Ale
    • Any beer tht is not a lager is an ale, but ale yeast ferments at warmer temperatures.
  • Rye
    • Cloudy and grainy tasting like a wheat beer, but rye adds "spiciness" to the brew.
  • Blonde
    • Middle-of-the-road, people-pleasing light beer with no overbearing malts or bitter hoppiness.
  • Stout
    • Heavily roasted grains give this ale its roasty coffee and chocolate notes... and of course its black color.
  • Pilsner
    • Light lager originating from the Czech city of Pilsen.
  • Hefeweizen
    • German-style wheat beer with strong notes of banana and clove.
